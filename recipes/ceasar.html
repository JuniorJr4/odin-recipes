Ceasar Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce - I typically use romain but any kind will work
  • 4 tbsp Olive oil
  • Half juiced lemon
  • 2 tbsp vinegar (white or apple cider)
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • Few dashes of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dry mustard or dijon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp crackd pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan

Method

  1. In a small bowl mix the garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, worcestershire sauce (if using dijon mix that in as well) and set aside for about 15 minutes for the flavors to infuse.
  2. In separate small bowl combine the parmesan, salt, pepper and dry mustard.
  3. Tear the lettuce in to desired size, wash thoroughly and dry.
  4. In a large bowl add the lettuce and mix in the dry and wet ingredients. Toss and serve immediately.

You can spice it up as you please, I typically add chopped bacon and/or chicken pieces, croutons and poached eggs to make it a main course.
