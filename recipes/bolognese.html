Bolognese Sauce

Ingredients for 6 portions

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 2tbsp butter
  • i cup fnely diced onions
  • 1/2 cup finely diced celery
  • 1/2 cup finely diced carrot
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • freshh ground black pepper and cayene to taste
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 lb ground meat(s) of choice, beef, pork, veal, italian sausage
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 cups white wine
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 2 cups water or broth of choice

Method

  1. Sautee onions, carrots and celery in butter and olive oil until onions are translucent. Be sure to add the salk at this stage to help remove the moisture.
  2. Add in the meat and cook while breaking the meat down into small pieces.
  3. Continue to cook down until most of the moisture from meat has cooked off.
  4. Now add in the milk and cook until most of the milk has cooked off. Be careful not to burn the milk!
  5. Add in the white wine and cook down again until most of the liquid has evorated.
  6. Add in the tomatoes and water/broth, be sure the tomatoes are crushed beforehand if using whole tomotoes.
  7. Turn the heat to low and simmer the sauce uncovered at least 1 hr but up to 6! The longer the better.
